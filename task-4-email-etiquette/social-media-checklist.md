# Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist

## Do's: 

- Think before you post, consider how it reflects on you
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
- Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal data
- Respect others online, be polite and avoid offensive content
- Balance your time, avoid excessive social media usage

## Don'ts: 

- Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, passwords, etc.)
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling
- Don’t believe or spread fake news blindly
- Don’t post inappropriate or harmful content
- Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people
